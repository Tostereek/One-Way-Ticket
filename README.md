# One Way Ticket
The player must resist the influence of the Solaris Ocean, which damages the spacecraft and causes visions or anomalies that pose a threat to the protagonist.
<br />

## General Overview
The player's objective is to keep the spacecraft operational until it reaches its destination. Unrepaired malfunctions reduce the spacecraft's speed, some bring it to a complete stop, and others result in a game over if they are not repaired within a limited time. Throughout the game, the player also experiences anomalies that gradually increase their insanity. Reaching 100% insanity also results in a game over.
<br />

## My part
My responsibilities include designing and building game levels, placing and polishing environmental assets, creating and implementing materials, and developing UI widgets.

The game features a cel-shaded art style with a limited color palette. The shader preserves key material properties, such as metallic and roughness values.
